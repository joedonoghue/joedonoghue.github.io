Ocean and terrain generation: JS, WebGL, Bump/Normal Mapping

This project involves projecting three differently-sized and seeded layers of Perlin noise on a 3D plane. A larger layer mimics the broad-shaped waves in the ocean or large hills and mountains. A second layer is for smaller details that create randomness and do away with a totally smooth wave or cliffside. The final layer is for fine detail like ripples in the water or a grainy/sandy land texture. Using a combination of parameters for these layers like amplitude and rate of change as well as color variations, you can come up with some cool looking ocean waves or terrain features.
